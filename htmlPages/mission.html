Welcome to Market to Market

Market to Market is a community-driven platform for small business owners seeking to create exponential growth through collaboration and exposure. At Market to Market, businesses can view upcoming local events and submit sponsorship proposals to event planners in exchange for exposure. By partnering with event planners, businesses owners have the opportunity to grow awareness of their brands and increase their market share by leveraging on the vast network of attendees.

Why use Market to Market?

Market to Market is a free platform that brings event planners and business owners together to create mutually beneficial relationships. While businesses get to generate value by tapping into immense networking opportunities and gaining exposure through sharing their products and services, event organizers increase the overall attendee satisfaction by providing access to additional products and services at no cost. Most importantly, Market to Market is absolutely free to use. All you have to do is sign up.
